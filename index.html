About me

Born and raised in San Diego, CA,
I moved to Portland, OR in 2014
to pursue an education in
communication sciences and disorders.
Although this didn't turn out
to be the field I ended up in,
I ended up working with children
under the autism spectrum,
making a difference in their
lives as they do mine.

These children over the years
have impacted my life,
teaching me patience and care
goes into everything that I do...

...which is why I started learning how to code!

I eventually want to make apps
and games for these kids,
but lack the skill to currently do so.
I have to start somewhere, so by
using tools on the internet,
I built this personal website!

I have experience now building with
HTML, CSS, jQuery and Javascript,
and hopefully in time I can
expand my knowledge to other
programming languages such as C++,
C# in the hopes of trying things
on the Unity engine.

In the meantime...
I also have a YouTube channel
that showcases science experiments,
vlogs, and such. This channel is
something I'm very proud of making.
Check it out if you have time!
