About me

Born and raised in San Diego, CA,
I moved to Portland, OR in 2014
to pursue an education in communication sciences and disorders.
Although it didn't become the career path I wanted,
I currently do work with children under the autism spectrum,
making a difference in their lives as they do mine.

These children over the years
have impacted my life,
teaching me patience and care
goes into everything that I do.
